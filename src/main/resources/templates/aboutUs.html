About us

Our goal is to create a platform to empower candidates

We empower candidates by impacting the labour market in a sustainable way.

Empower candidates

Adroit's main focus is to empower candidates and impact the job-market in a positive way. We do this by utilizing modern tech and the experience and resources within the AW Family.

Matching candidates

Through our matching-algorithms we connect the most relevant candidates to the employers need of competence of a specific job-position. This is mainly based on qualifications needed and benefits offered.

Sustainable labour market

We believe in making a positive impact on the labour market. This is made by working against discrimination of candidates. A company can only see a full profile after candidate accepts an offer.
