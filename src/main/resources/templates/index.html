How Adroit works



STEP 1

Create a profile

Together with our team at Academic Work you will get a professional profile at Adroit. We take photos, help write your competences and together fill in your preferences. Your picture and name will be hidden from companies so that only your competences and preferences are shown.




STEP 2

Get offers from companies

Based on your qualifications and preferences you will be matched with employers. Here the companies has the opportunity to read your preferences to give an individual offer to further attract your competences.




STEP 3

Make a choice

If you chose to accept an offer the employer will gain access to your full profile.
